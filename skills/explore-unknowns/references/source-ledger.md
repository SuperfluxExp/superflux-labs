## Preserved ideas

The skill preserves these source-level ideas:

1. The map is not the territory.
2. Unknowns are the gap between prompt/plan/context and real work constraints.
3. Model output quality is bottlenecked by the user's ability to clarify unknowns.
4. Unknowns show up before, during, and after implementation.
5. Four useful buckets:
   - known knowns;
   - known unknowns;
   - unknown knowns;
   - unknown unknowns.
6. Useful techniques:
   - blindspot pass;
   - brainstorming/prototyping;
   - one-question-at-a-time interviews;
   - reference implementations/artifacts;
   - decision-first implementation plans;
   - implementation notes;
   - post-change proof reports and quizzes.
